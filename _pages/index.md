Hi, I'm June, a Business Analytics graduate based in Perth WA, who genuinely enjoys figuring out what's behind the numbers and helping teams make better decisions because of it.

My most meaningful experience so far has been with the Newcastle Knights Commercial Team, where I got to do real analyst work — benchmarking 20+ membership models, digging into pricing and engagement data with Power BI and Excel, and pulling it all together into a Business Club proposal that projected a 20% improvement in corporate member retention. It was the kind of project where I had to think like a business person, not just an analyst, and I loved that.

Since finishing my degree, I've been keeping the momentum going — working through certifications in Power BI, Agile Analysis, and Excel, and sharpening my Python and SQL skills, because I want to be someone who can handle the technical side just as confidently as the business conversation.

On a personal note — I travel whenever I can, and I speak four languages. These things might sound unrelated to analytics, but honestly, they've shaped how I think about people and context, which I find makes a real difference when you're trying to understand what a stakeholder actually needs, or communicate findings to someone who doesn't live in spreadsheets.

I'm looking for a graduate or entry-level role where I can contribute from day one, keep learning, and grow into a well-rounded analyst. If that sounds like a fit, I'd love to connect.
